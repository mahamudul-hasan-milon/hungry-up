Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Delivery typically takes 30-45 minutes depending on your location and traffic conditions. During peak hours, it might take up to 60 minutes.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, credit/debit cards, and mobile banking (bKash, Nagad, Rocket). Online payment is also available through our app.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel your order within 5 minutes of placing it without any charge. After that, a cancellation fee may apply if the food preparation has started.

Do you deliver to my area?

We deliver to most areas in Dhaka. Enter your address during checkout to check if we deliver to your location. We're constantly expanding our delivery zones.
